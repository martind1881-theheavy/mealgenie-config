Privacy Policy
Meal Genie

Chrome Extension by Innov8Edge  ·  AI-Powered Culinary Assistant

Last Updated: March 2026
Quick Summary

Meal Genie stores everything locally on your device. Your API keys (OpenAI, Claude, Gemini, Groq) are saved only in your browser's encrypted storage and are sent exclusively to the AI provider you choose when generating recipes. Generated recipe images are stored only in your browser's local IndexedDB. We never operate our own servers, never collect your data, and never sell or share anything about you.

📋 1. Information We Collect

Meal Genie collects and stores the following information locally on your device only:

API Keys

  • OpenAI API key (for recipe generation and DALL-E image generation)
  • Anthropic Claude API key (optional alternative AI provider)
  • Google Gemini API key (optional alternative AI provider)
  • Groq API key (optional alternative AI provider)

All keys are stored in Chrome's encrypted chrome.storage.local. Innov8Edge never sees, receives, or stores your API keys.

Recipe History

  • Ingredients you entered, recipes generated, and the date they were created
  • Stored locally in chrome.storage.local, capped automatically to prevent excessive storage use

Generated Images

  • AI-generated food images (via DALL-E 3) are cached locally in your browser's IndexedDB (MealGenieImages database)
  • Images are pruned automatically when no longer associated with a current recipe
  • No images are uploaded to or stored on Innov8Edge servers

Usage & Preferences

  • Active AI provider selection and failover preferences
  • Free generation count (for free-tier usage limits)
  • Subscription status verified via BrowserBill

We do not collect your name, email address, location, browsing history, dietary health data, or any information beyond what is listed above.

⚙️ 2. How We Use Your Information

  • Recipe Generation: Your ingredients and preferences are assembled into a prompt and sent to your chosen AI provider to generate recipes
  • Image Generation: If you have an OpenAI key saved, recipe titles are used to generate food photography via DALL-E 3
  • Image Fallback: When AI image generation is unavailable, Meal Genie fetches relevant food photos from Unsplash or Pexels using the recipe title as a search term
  • Discover Tab: Your local recipe history is displayed back to you within the extension — it never leaves your device
  • Provider Failover: If your active AI provider fails, Meal Genie can automatically switch to another saved provider to ensure uninterrupted recipe generation
  • Subscription Verification: BrowserBill is used to verify whether you are on the free or premium tier

💾 4. Data Storage

Local Storage Only

All user data is stored on your device using two browser-native mechanisms:

  • chrome.storage.local — API keys, preferences, recipe history, and usage counts
  • IndexedDB (MealGenieImages) — Cached food images generated by DALL-E or fetched from Unsplash/Pexels

No Cloud Sync

Meal Genie does not operate its own servers and does not back up your data to any cloud service. Your recipes, images, and keys exist only on the device where the extension is installed.

Automatic Pruning

Recipe history is capped automatically — older entries are removed when the limit is reached. Cached images are pruned whenever they are no longer associated with a current recipe session, keeping your local storage lean.

Clearing Your Data

You can delete all locally stored data at any time via the extension's settings panel. Removing the extension from Chrome also clears all associated storage automatically.

🚫 6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties.

Data is transmitted externally only in these specific cases:

  • Your chosen AI provider's API — when you click Generate to create recipes
  • OpenAI DALL-E 3 — when AI image generation is enabled and you have an OpenAI key
  • Unsplash or Pexels — a recipe title search query when fetching free fallback food photos
  • BrowserBill — to verify your subscription status

No analytics platforms, advertising networks, data brokers, or Innov8Edge servers receive any information from this extension.

🔒 8. Data Security

  • HTTPS encryption for all communications with AI providers, image services, and BrowserBill
  • Local-first architecture — your data stays on your device by default
  • API keys stored in Chrome's encrypted chrome.storage.local
  • No server-side processing, logging, or analytics by Innov8Edge
  • IndexedDB image cache is sandboxed to the extension's origin and inaccessible to websites or other extensions
